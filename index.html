Japan is an island nation in East Asia known for its unique blend of ancient traditions and cutting-edge technology. Famous for its temples, cherry blossoms, samurai history, and sushi, Japan offers a rich cultural tapestry rooted in Shinto and Buddhist beliefs. It is also a global leader in innovation, robotics, and animation.
